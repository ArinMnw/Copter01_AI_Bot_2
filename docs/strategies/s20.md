# Strategy 20 (All in 4s - Pipeline Architecture)

## Overview
Strategy 20 (S20) is an advanced custom implementation based on the **"All in 4s"** methodology taught by P'Nai. The strategy has been refactored into a **3-Stage Pipeline Architecture** to combine deep Price Action concepts, structural traps, and psychological targets into a robust trading system.

## The 3-Stage Pipeline Architecture

### Stage 1: Base Triggers (สัญญาณเข้าออเดอร์หลัก)
The strategy scans the market structure for specific high-probability setups. Each trigger can be enabled or disabled independently.
1. **Defect Pullback (ตำหนิ):** Identifies a counter-trend candle followed by a trend candle that *fails to engulf the previous wick*. Price must return to "fix" this defect.
2. **2L/2H Trap (โครงสร้าง 2L 2H):** Searches for a massive rejection Anchor candle. When price pulls back to the 50%-60% zone of the body without breaking the extreme wick, it forms a Double Low (2L) or Double High (2H) exhaustion structure.
3. **Solid Candle Break:** Detects strong momentum breakouts (Marubozu/Solid candles) breaking significant Swings.
4. **FVG Retrace:** Identifies Fair Value Gaps aligned with the trend and enters on the retracement.

### Stage 2: Modifiers (ตัวกรองความแม่นยำ)
Once a Base Trigger is found, Modifiers refine the signal and add confluence to ensure higher win rates.
1. **Magic Number 7:** Front-runs psychological round numbers (e.g., `00`, `50`). Adjusts Take Profit targets to end in the digit `7` (The Magic Number for ATH) or `3` to avoid liquidity sweeps.
2. **No Body Close (No Body Break):** Ensures that the retracement candles do not close with their body beyond the Anchor candle's critical zones.
3. **Fibo Confluence (นัยยะสำคัญ):** Checks if the entry aligns with major Fibonacci retracement levels (e.g., 61.8%, 78.6%) of the macro swing.

### Stage 3: Dynamic Exits (การจัดการเป้าหมาย TP/SL)
The strategy dynamically calculates the Take Profit and Stop Loss based on the origin of the Base Trigger.
- **Defect Fibo Run:** If the setup originates from a Defect trigger, it targets a deeper psychological Fibo level: `RUN (7.467)`.
- **Standard Fibo Run:** For 2L/2H and other triggers, it targets the standard strong momentum Fibo: `RUN (7.044)`.
- **The "No Touch" SL Rule:** Stop Loss is placed strictly at the tip of the Anchor's extreme wick + a small `S20_SL_2L2H` buffer. If price touches this wick, the setup is invalidated.

## Configuration Variables (`config.py`)
- `S20_ENABLED`: Master toggle for the entire S20 strategy.
- **Triggers:** `S20_TRIGGER_DEFECT`, `S20_TRIGGER_2L2H`, `S20_TRIGGER_SOLID`, `S20_TRIGGER_FVG`
- **Modifiers:** `S20_MODIFIER_MAGIC_NUM`, `S20_MODIFIER_NO_BODY_BRK`, `S20_MODIFIER_FIBO_CONF`
- **Settings:** `S20_ENTRY_BUFFER` (ระยะเผื่อเข้า), `S20_SL_2L2H` (ระยะเผื่อ SL)
