# Vannevar Bush

**Vannevar Bush** (1890–1974) was an American engineer, inventor, and science administrator.

## Relevance to this wiki

Bush is significant as the originator of the [[memex]] concept, described in his 1945 essay *"As We May Think"* (The Atlantic). The Memex envisioned a personal knowledge store with **associative trails** — user-created links between documents — that is the intellectual ancestor of the [[llm-wiki-pattern]].

## Key contribution

Bush proposed that the critical challenge of the information age was not storage but **selection and association** — finding connections between pieces of knowledge. His solution was a personal device that would make these connections persistent and shareable.

The limitation of Bush's vision was that the **user** had to create and maintain all the associative trails manually. The [[wiki-maintenance-burden]] made this impractical at scale. The LLM Wiki Pattern addresses this gap by delegating maintenance to an LLM.
